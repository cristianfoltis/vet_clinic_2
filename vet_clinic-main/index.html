McCauley Animal Clinic

At McCauley Animal Clinic and New Melle Animal Hospital, we strive to deliver a relaxed, "family style" atmosphere, and establish an intimate relationship with you and your pets. We look forward to serving all of your pet's lifetime health care needs, and becoming part of your extended family!

About Us

Taking care of your pet can be a very large task.We get it! That's why New Melle Animal Hospital is happy to assist you every step of the way. Since 1971, we have been offering your pets the highest quality care in the Newbury Park area at prices you can afford. We strive to work with your budget to ensure that your pet gets the best veterinary treatments possible, regardless of your income.
